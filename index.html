  I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Rutgers University focusing on Machine Learning, advised by Prof. Sungjin Ahn. 

The research questions I am interested in are:
  • How can we infer structured representations of data without supervision? 
  • Since organisms are endowed with innate priors, what kind of innateness can we bake into our models to achieve this?
  • Do the inferred representations also have some causal interpretation?
In exploring these, I rely on building probabilistic models using variational approximations and deep learning. 

Prior to starting a Ph.D. in 2018, I worked at IBM Research India for 3 years after obtaining a B.Tech. from IIT Guwahati in 2015
